The Founders

Becket

Currently a Senior at Tufts University in Boston, Becket is the dreamer behind TeemPlay. Becket
excels at seeing the bigger picture and he uses that vision to bring the whole team to a higher level!
With Becket leading, the team really is much greater than the sum of its parts. With unyielding optimism
and work ethic Becket gives TeemPlay the structure it will need to succeed. Becket looks forward to the
day when he can pass down the mantle, and vision, of TeemPlay to the players! He believes that,
together, we can build a game that will change the world!

becket@teemplay.com

Willis

As a Junior at UNC Chapel Hill Willis is the true nerd behind TeemPlay’s success to this point! Willis
has always loved video games but his passion doesn’t stop at just playing. Instead Willis spends an
enormous amount of time reading and researching everything about games from programming game
mechanics to market analytics to emerging trends in gamer psychology. Willis keeps the rest of the
team in line when they try to play fast-and-loose with facts or misquote a statistic. Willis isn’t negitive
but when he says something like “eh I guess that will work” the whole team celebrates.


willis@teemplay.com

Steve

Currently a Junior at Florida Atlantic University (FAU) Steve could be characterized as the “try-hard”
of the team. In high school Steve was captain of the swim team and #1 at the state meet. People
tremble in the wake of Steve’s video game avatars as his competitive nature spills into the virtual
world! Steve brings that same fiery passion that he uses to dominate games to TeemPlay. Because of
this passion, if Steve doesn’t know something… he goes out and teaches himself. In this way Steve
has given himself a crash course in: Unity, the CryEngine, Sony Vegas, and a long list of other odds
and ends which made our first demo video possible.

steve@teemplay.com
